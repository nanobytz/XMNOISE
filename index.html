Story.

Give me the songs of a nation, I care not who writes its laws. - Andrew Fletcher

It all started on 3/4/2001 at the Tropicana Dome in Tampa, Fl when a new dimension of music was discovered. Music is powerful. Encompassing the imagination, the feelings of the heart and the passion of the soul in a single medium.

This time however, something was different. Transcending the physical construct of just sound waves and fine instrumentation. It felt like these were ancient sounds, birthed into the very fabric of the cosmos. We exist to re-discover that sound and share the experience with humanity.

Discover the Song of All Songs within your own soul. Eternity has been placed in our hearts. We humbly share our own attempt with you.
